The Fetchbot II design requires a female GPIO header mounted on the reverse side of a Raspberry Pi Zero W. That makes most pinout guides confusing to use as a reference, so here's a backwards one.

  1. Blue LED
  2. LED common ground
  3. Green LED
  4. Red LED
  5. IN4 (left forward)
  6. IN3 (left backward)
  7. IN2 (right forward)
  8. IN1 (right backward)
